About Me

Anurag Vartak is a passionate storyteller and explorer with a deep connection to tradition and nature. Born in Sawantwadi and raised in Kannur, Kerala, Anurag blends modern curiosity with the timeless wisdom of Sanatana Dharma. An ABAP developer by profession, he finds balance between technology and spirituality, often capturing the essence of temples, sunsets, beaches, and everyday life for his YouTube channel Anurag Unfiltered. A Pisces with a creative soul, Anurag is on a continuous journey to grow — whether it’s seeking a lean, healthy lifestyle, perfecting his skin health, or discovering the hidden gems of India’s coastlines.
