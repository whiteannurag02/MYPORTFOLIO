My Top 3 Movies which are best of all time

The Best movies according to Annurag

Here are my top 3 movies that I believe are the best of all time:

Dhoom 2 (2006)

- Directed by Sanjay Gadhvi, this action-packed thriller features Hrithik Roshan and Aishwarya Rai in lead roles. The film is known for its stunning visuals, high-octane action sequences, and a gripping storyline that keeps the audience on the edge of their seats.

3 idiots (2009)

- Directed by Rajkumar Hirani, this comedy-drama stars Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, and Sharman Joshi. The film is a heartwarming tale about friendship, the pressures of education, and the importance of following one's passion.

The Dark Knight (2008)

- Directed by Christopher Nolan, this superhero film features Christian Bale as Batman and Heath Ledger as the Joker. The film is renowned for its complex characters, moral dilemmas, and Ledger's iconic performance that earned him a posthumous Academy Award.
